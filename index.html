We are GoodLives GM

We are a system shifting capability for Greater Manchester working to reduce inequalities by tackling system barriers to embedding and spreading community-focused innovation in GM

Across Greater Manchester we find many examples of brilliant community focused innovation tackling inequalities.

But the very systems that are intended to help people can sometimes create barriers to transformative work like this, reducing its impact and even bringing it to a standstill.

If community focused innovation tackling inequalities is to flourish we need to name the system barriers we encounter, for example in our leadership and governance arrangements, in how people work together, and in how money and other resources are distributed.

Barriers like these can be shifted. Systems can be enablers.

With focus and collective action we can change our systems so they work better for the people of Greater Manchester - and for us.

About GoodLives GM

GoodLives GM is a system shifting capability for Greater Manchester working to reduce inequalities by tackling system barriers to embedding and spreading community focused innovation in GM.

GoodLives GM grew from a series of conversations with brave leaders and brilliant innovators across Greater Manchester who identified common barriers to embedding this kind of innovation and shared our ambition to shift them. These people lead powerful work like Elephant's Trail, Living Well, GM Moving and Healthy Hyde, and we explored with them how a system shifting capability could help them tackle the system barriers they experience.
